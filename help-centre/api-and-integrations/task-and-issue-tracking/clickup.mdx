Using ClickUp to track your team's tasks? Want to export [your incident follow-ups](/help-centre/creating-and-running-incidents/managing-ongoing-incidents/keeping-track-of-who's-doing-what) to ClickUp to keep everything centralised?

This is how.

---

## What we can do with ClickUp

Our current integration is a fairly simple setup for now. In its present state, the integration lets you:

1. **Export** [your incident follow-ups](/help-centre/creating-and-running-incidents/managing-ongoing-incidents/keeping-track-of-who's-doing-what) to ClickUp; and
2. **Sync the status of your tasks between ClickUp and incident.io** , so you can work in ClickUp without worrying about updating statuses manually in incident.io!
3. [Auto-export follow-ups](/help-centre/api-and-integrations/task-and-issue-tracking/auto-exporting-follow-ups)  to ClickUp, based on pre-configured rules

## Getting Set Up

1. **Go to Settings >** [Integrations](https://app.incident.io/settings/integrations)

Hit `Install` on ClickUp.

![](/images/help-centre/clickup/screenshot-1.png)

2. **Generate a ClickUp Access Token**

Log in to ClickUp, click on your profile photo in the bottom-left, then **Apps** . Generate a API Token, and copy it to your clipboard.

![](/images/help-centre/clickup/screenshot-2.png)

Now head back to the incident.io dashboard.

3. **Paste the generated API key into the incident.io pop-up**

![](/images/help-centre/clickup/screenshot-3.png)

That's it

If you run into any issues, [get in touch](http://incident.io/community)
