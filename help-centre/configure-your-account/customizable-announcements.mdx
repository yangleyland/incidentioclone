We've added more customization options to the announcement posts! Yes, this includes customizing the emojis for each field or action that appears in the post

To customize Announcements you can head over to the [announcements settings](https://app.incident.io/~/settings/announcements)

![](/images/help-centre/customizable-announcements/screenshot-1.png)

Click on "Edit Announcement post" to further customize the post.

![](/images/help-centre/customizable-announcements/screenshot-2.png)

You can add more Fields, or more Actions and will see a preview of the announcement post message as you configure it!

![](/images/help-centre/customizable-announcements/screenshot-3.png)

You can re-order fields, delete or add them, and even configure which emojis appear next to them

![](/images/help-centre/customizable-announcements/screenshot-4.png)

Learn more about how to create Announcement rules in this [article](/help-centre/creating-and-running-incidents/faqs/can-we-change-where-incidents-get-announced)
