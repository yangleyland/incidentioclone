People can subscribe to status pages by email, or through our RSS or Atom feeds. When you select "Subscribe to updates", you see a form like this, which allows you to choose whether to subscribe to specific components:

![subscribe.png](/images/help-centre/status-page-subscriptions/screenshot-1.png)

When sending a status page update, you can choose whether to notify subscribers.

<Info>

If you choose to notify subscribers, updates will still appear in the RSS and Atom feeds for the incident.

</Info>
