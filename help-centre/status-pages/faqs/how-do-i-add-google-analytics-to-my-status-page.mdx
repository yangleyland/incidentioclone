## Context

When managing a public status page, you may want to track visitor analytics using Google Analytics to better understand your page traffic and usage patterns.

## Answer

You can add Google Analytics tracking to your public status page through the page settings. Here's how:

1. Navigate to Status Pages in your dashboard;
2. Select your public status page;
3. Click on **Settings** ;
4. Go to **Basic Settings** ;
5. Under the **Customisation** section, locate the **Google Analytics tag** field;
6. Add your Google Analytics tag.
