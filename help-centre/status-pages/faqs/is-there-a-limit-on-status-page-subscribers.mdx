There is no limit on the number of subscribers who can sign up to receive status page updates for your organization. You can have as many email and Slack subscribers as needed.

To view and manage your current Status Page subscribers:

1. Navigate to your Status Page settings in the incident.io app
2. Go to the "Subscriptions" tab
3. Here you can view and manage all current subscribers
